FAQ

Do I need prior experience to enroll?+
No experience needed for our beginner courses. Just a laptop, internet, and the desire to learn. We start from the very basics.
How are classes delivered?+
Pre-recorded video lessons you watch at your own pace, plus weekly live Zoom sessions for Q&A and code reviews. Everything is accessible 24/7.
What payment methods are accepted?+
Card payments, bank transfer, and USSD for Nigerian students. International students can pay by card. All payments are secured via Paystack.

Step 1 is free for everyone to preview. Steps 2–5 and all full course content unlock after payment to ensure enrolled students get the full structured curriculum, live support, and certificate.
